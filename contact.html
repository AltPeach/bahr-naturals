Frequently Asked Questions

How are your soaps different from commercial brands?

Our soaps are handcrafted in small batches using traditional South Sudanese beauty wisdom combined with premium Canadian ingredients. We cure each bar for 6 weeks and use only natural, ethically sourced materials.

Do you offer custom soap orders?

Yes! We love creating custom soaps for special occasions, weddings, or corporate gifts. Contact us with your requirements and we'll create something unique that honors both your needs and our heritage traditions.

Can I visit your soap-making studio?

We offer studio visits by appointment where you can see our soap-making process and learn about South Sudanese beauty traditions. Please call ahead to schedule your visit.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently we ship within Canada with free shipping on orders over $50. We're working on international shipping options - please contact us for special requests.

Are your products suitable for sensitive skin?

Our soaps are made with natural ingredients and are generally suitable for sensitive skin. Our Gentle Baby soap is specifically formulated for the most sensitive skin types. If you have specific allergies, please review our ingredient lists or contact us.

How do I care for handmade soap?

Keep your soap in a dry place between uses, preferably on a soap dish that allows drainage. Our cotton soap bags help extend the life of each bar. With proper care, each soap lasts 4-6 weeks.
