Honoring Ancient Wisdom

Founded by Ester Simon, Bahr Naturals bridges the beauty traditions of South Sudan with the pristine natural ingredients of Canada.

Our name "Bahr" means "river" in Arabic – symbolizing the life-giving waters of the Nile that have nourished South Sudanese culture for millennia. Each soap is crafted with authentic shea butter and botanical essences, honoring traditional recipes while meeting modern standards of purity and sustainability.
