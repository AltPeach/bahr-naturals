Our Heritage Story

A journey of tradition, resilience, and the beauty that connects us across cultures

"Every bar of soap carries the wisdom of my grandmothers and the promise of new beginnings"

— Ester Simon

Meet Ester Simon

Born on the banks of the White Nile in South Sudan, I learned the ancient art of natural skincare from my grandmother, who taught me that beauty comes from honoring both the earth and our heritage.

Childhood by the Nile

Growing up in South Sudan, I watched my grandmother create healing balms and cleansing bars from shea nuts and aromatic herbs. These weren't just beauty products – they were sacred rituals connecting us to our ancestors and the land.

Journey to Canada

Like many South Sudanese families, we sought new opportunities in Canada. Calgary became our new home, but I carried with me the precious knowledge of traditional beauty practices and a deep longing to share our cultural wisdom.

Rediscovering Heritage

In my Calgary kitchen, I began recreating my grandmother's recipes, blending authentic South Sudanese ingredients with the pristine botanicals of Canada. Each bar became a bridge between two homes, two cultures, one beautiful tradition.

Bahr Naturals is Born

"Bahr" means river in Arabic – the life-giving waters that nourished our ancestors. Today, Bahr Naturals flows from my heart to yours, carrying forward generations of wisdom while embracing the future of natural, sustainable beauty.

Our Craftsmanship Process

Where ancient techniques meet modern standards of excellence

1

Heritage Selection

We carefully source authentic shea butter and traditional South Sudanese botanicals, working with trusted suppliers who understand the cultural significance of each ingredient.

2

Traditional Preparation

Following ancestral methods passed down through generations, we prepare each ingredient with the reverence and care that honors our cultural heritage and natural wisdom.

3

Mindful Blending

In our Calgary studio, Canadian botanicals are thoughtfully combined with traditional ingredients, creating harmonious blends that bridge two worlds of natural beauty.

4

Hand Crafting

Each bar is hand-poured and shaped with care, ensuring consistent quality while maintaining the personal touch that makes every soap a unique expression of our heritage.

5

Patient Curing

Our soaps cure naturally for 4-6 weeks in controlled conditions, allowing the ingredients to fully harmonize and develop the rich, gentle qualities our customers love.

6

Heritage Packaging

Finally, each soap is wrapped with care in sustainable packaging that reflects our cultural aesthetics while protecting the environment for future generations.

Honoring South Sudanese Beauty Traditions

Ancient wisdom meets modern care in every aspect of our practice

Shea Butter Wisdom

For centuries, South Sudanese women have treasured shea butter as nature's perfect moisturizer. We honor this tradition by using only the finest, ethically-sourced shea butter in our formulations.

Aromatic Botanicals

Our ancestors understood the healing power of aromatic herbs. We carefully select botanicals that echo these traditional remedies while meeting modern purity standards.

Sacred Rituals

Beauty care in South Sudanese culture is deeply ritualistic, connecting us to ancestors and community. Each bar of our soap carries forward this sacred approach to self-care.
