Meet Ester Simon

Roots in South Sudan

Born in Juba, South Sudan, Ester grew up surrounded by the rich traditions of her Dinka heritage. Her grandmother, a respected healer and keeper of ancient beauty secrets, taught her the sacred art of creating natural skincare remedies using shea butter, indigenous plants, and the pure waters of the Nile.

A Journey to Canada

Like many South Sudanese families, Ester's journey to Canada was one of hope and new beginnings. Settling in Toronto in 2018, she carried with her not only memories of home but also the precious knowledge passed down through generations of women in her family.

Bridging Two Worlds

Recognizing the incredible potential of combining traditional South Sudanese beauty wisdom with Canada's pristine natural resources, Ester began crafting soaps in her kitchen. Each recipe honors her heritage while embracing the quality and sustainability standards her new home offers.
