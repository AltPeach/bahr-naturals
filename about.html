Our Heritage Story

From the banks of the Nile to the heart of Canada - a journey of resilience, tradition, and the timeless wisdom of natural beauty.

"Every bar of soap carries the spirit of my grandmother's wisdom and the hope of preserving our beautiful traditions for future generations."

Ester Simon
Founder & Artisan

Meet Ester Simon

Roots in South Sudan

Born in Juba, South Sudan, Ester grew up surrounded by the rich traditions of her Dinka heritage. Her grandmother, a respected healer and keeper of ancient beauty secrets, taught her the sacred art of creating natural skincare remedies using shea butter, indigenous plants, and the pure waters of the Nile.

A Journey to Canada

Like many South Sudanese families, Ester's journey to Canada was one of hope and new beginnings. Settling in Toronto in 2018, she carried with her not only memories of home but also the precious knowledge passed down through generations of women in her family.

Bridging Two Worlds

Recognizing the incredible potential of combining traditional South Sudanese beauty wisdom with Canada's pristine natural resources, Ester began crafting soaps in her kitchen. Each recipe honors her heritage while embracing the quality and sustainability standards her new home offers.

A Mission of Preservation

Today, Bahr Naturals stands as more than a business—it's a cultural bridge, a way to share the beauty traditions of South Sudan with the world, and a means to preserve precious knowledge for future generations of both South Sudanese and Canadian communities.

Honoring Our Heritage

🌊

The Nile's Gift

In South Sudanese tradition, the Nile River represents life, purity, and renewal. Our soap-making process honors this sacred connection to water, using the purest Canadian spring water in tribute to the life-giving waters of our homeland.

🌰

Shea Butter Wisdom

For centuries, South Sudanese women have treasured shea butter as nature's perfect moisturizer. Passed down through generations, the knowledge of selecting and preparing this golden gift forms the heart of our skincare philosophy.

🎨

Traditional Patterns

The geometric patterns found in South Sudanese beadwork and textiles inspire our packaging design. Each element respectfully represents the artistic heritage while creating a modern, accessible aesthetic.

👥

Community Spirit

Ubuntu - "I am because we are." This South Sudanese philosophy of interconnectedness drives our commitment to community, sustainability, and sharing the benefits of our success with both Canadian and South Sudanese communities.

🌱

Healing Plants

Traditional South Sudanese medicine relies on the healing properties of indigenous plants. We honor this knowledge by carefully selecting botanicals that offer similar benefits, bridging ancient wisdom with modern skincare science.

✨

Ritual & Ceremony

In South Sudanese culture, beauty rituals are sacred ceremonies of self-care and community bonding. Our products are crafted to transform your daily routine into a mindful, heritage-honoring experience.

Our Artisan Process

Each soap is handcrafted using traditional methods enhanced by modern quality standards

1

Ingredient Selection

We carefully source the finest natural ingredients, prioritizing organic Canadian suppliers while honoring traditional South Sudanese components like authentic shea butter.

2

Traditional Mixing

Following recipes passed down through generations, we blend ingredients by hand in small batches, maintaining the personal touch that machine production cannot replicate.

3

Cultural Blessing

Each batch receives a traditional blessing in Dinka, honoring the ancestors and infusing our soaps with positive intentions for healing and beauty.

4

Patient Curing

Our soaps cure for a full 6 weeks, following traditional timing that ensures optimal hardness, longevity, and the development of rich, creamy lather.

5

Quality Testing

Every batch undergoes rigorous testing for pH balance, consistency, and skin-friendliness, ensuring Canadian quality standards while maintaining traditional effectiveness.

6

Heritage Packaging

Finally, each soap is lovingly wrapped in eco-friendly packaging featuring traditional patterns, ready to bring heritage and healing to your home.
